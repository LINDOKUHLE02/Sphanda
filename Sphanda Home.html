Sphanda


Sphanda is a small up coming transportation service available for anyone and any place around South Africa.This is a private company which has been running successfully since the 8th of March 2018, it developed in the township of Ackerville Witbank in Mpumalanga.A company lead by a very innovative and passionate man Sibusiso Sizwe Monana.

The individuals passion is highlighted by the growth of business of the last couple of years. During the period of Covid19 the company was at a trough as well with the country but still managed to survive as a small up coming in South Africa. This shows individual brilliance and self motivated drive from Sibusiso Monana. Not only does Sphanda do nation wide services but it is also a school transport around Witbank waiting to expand.
